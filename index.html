1. What is Go Play Chess?

Answer: Go Play Chess is where you can play free online real time chess with other players around the world from the comfort of your own browser.

2. Cool, so how do I start playing free chess?

Answer: All you need to do is make an account, activate your account through your email and login to start playing.

3. Do I need to install any browser plugins or software?

Answer: Nope. All you need is a modern browser that supports web sockets. You will also need JavaScript enabled. Go Play Chess has been tested on the latest versions in Edge, IE, Firefox, Chrome and Opera. Desktop, laptop, tablet, and mobile devices are all supported.

4. Do I need to pay money to play chess on this site?

Answer: Nope, its completely free.
